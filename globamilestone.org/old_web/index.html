We are the Global Milestone Club

A free and accessible tutoring service for a just world.

Our Mission

Accessible Tutoring

Access to a myriad of subjects; just a click away for students around the world.

Student Driven

Lessons for students, by students; building experiences that benefit all.

Global Connections

Uniting the world through equitable online education without borders.

How It Works

Lessons over Zoom

We currently provide all of our tutoring over the Zoom video meeting platform. Alternatively, students can connect via other platforms if needed.

Weekly Classes

Classes in the four core subjects: Writing, Reading, History, and Math are once per week. Special subject classes happen once per month.

Graded Homework & Exams

In addition to online classes, we provide practice worksheets, assignments, and exams to solidify students' understanding.
